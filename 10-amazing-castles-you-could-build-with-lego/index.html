Castles have always been a source of fascination for people. If you’re like most people, your mind probably wanders to images of imposing stone fortresses with soaring turrets and winding staircases. They’re majestic, imposing structures that seem to be straight out of a fairytale. While it’s definitely fun to imagine yourself living in one of these fairy-tale-like structures, few of us have the time or money to actually build one. Or do we? Believe it or not, you can create some pretty amazing castles using nothing but LEGO blocks!

Here are some amazing castles you can put together using LEGO bricks.

10 Mini Disney Castle

Miniature versions of popular Disney castles can now be built with LEGO sets! This new release by LEGO is sure to please both young and old Disney fans alike. The set includes smaller versions of iconic Disney castles. Assembly is easy and requires no glue or special tools, so even novice builders can enjoy putting these charming little castles together. With over 500 pieces in each set, there is plenty to keep everyone entertained for hours on end. Kids will have a blast building their own tiny worlds based on some of their favorite Disney. Order your set today.

As toddlers role-play their favorite characters from “Frozen,” they improve language skills while developing social-emotional awareness. Open up some creative possibilities by creating stories around Olaf, who lights up each corner of this magical castle at night when you press his button on top–creating endless dreamy moments together. Order your set today.

Plus, it comes with a handy carrying case so you can take your creations with you wherever you go. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced LEGO builder, the Crafting Box 3.0 set has something for everyone. So what are you waiting for? Order your set today.

Now, thanks to LEGO, you can build your own medieval castle! This incredible set comes with over 1,400 pieces, including a working drawbridge and detachable turrets. Have you ever wondered what it would be like to visit the Hogwarts™ Astronomy Tower? Well, thanks to the new LEGO Harry Potter set, now you can! This detailed and faithfully recreated set offers a unique opportunity to explore one of the most iconic locations in the Harry Potter series. 3 The Ice Castle

The LEGO Group released the “Ice Castle” LEGO set in honor of Disney’s Frozen. The set has been incredibly popular, selling out within hours both online and in stores. This iconic set is based on the castle in Arendelle that features prominently in the movie. It includes Elsa, Anna, Olaf, and Kristoff Minifigures, as well as four snowmen and two sleighs. If you’re a Frozen fan, this is a must-have set! Order your set today.

If you’re a fan of both LEGO and Disney, then you’re going to love this new set. The Disney Castle LEGO is a faithfully recreated replica of the iconic castle from the theme parks, and it’s sure to be a hit with fans young and old. With over 4,000 pieces, it’s the perfect project for builders of all skill levels. Plus, the finished product is just plain gorgeous! 1 Hogwarts Castle

When it comes to LEGO, there’s no building toy more iconic than Hogwarts™ Castle. The majestic castle has been recreated in LEGO form time and time again, with each iteration getting more and more impressive. This detailed set allows fans of all ages to recreate their favorite moments from the Harry Potter movies, or come up with their own magical adventures.
