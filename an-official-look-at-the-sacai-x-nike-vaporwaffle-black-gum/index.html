An Official Look At The Sacai x Nike VaporWaffle “Black Gum”

The creative and fashionable mind of Chitose Abe has been entrusted to continue impacting the space with innovative propositions since she introduced her first series of footwear collaborations with Nike in May 2019. The two companies decided to release more products for sneakerheads in 2022, including the sacai x Nike VaporWaffle “Black/Gum,”.

After months of speculation and leaked images, we’ve finally gotten our hands on official photographs of the highly anticipated sacai x Nike VaporWaffle “Black/Gum”. This one will be hitting shelves soon, so you better get your hands on these before they sell out again like all of those other times (you know what we mean).

The popular silhouette has been decked out in a stealthy-looking pair that should appeal to anyone who wants something more wearable than most others seen thus far – which is why this particular design by Chitose Abe at her label deserves attention from sneakerheads everywhere.

The unique design of these coveted sneakers is unparalleled and can be yours for an affordable price. Crafted from a variety of fabrics, the upper consists primarily of suede with some canvas thrown into the mix to really make it pop! And because this collaboration between Nike and sacai has been going on since LDWaffle days.

Order at Nike.com.

Along with the “White/Sail” pair, these are expected to be released in the coming months. The price is set to the usual $180 USD.

In the meantime, check out Nike Men’s VaporWaffle DD1875 500 Sacai – Dark Iris available to buy on Amazon.
