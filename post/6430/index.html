30 Celebs Who Are Obsessed With Their Dogs

It’s no secret that dogs are man’s best friend. When it comes to celebrity dog lovers, there seems to be no shortage of them. In fact, a quick look at social media will reveal that many famous people are more than happy to share photos and videos of their furry friends.

Whether they’re cuddling with them or taking them on walks, these stars just can’t seem to get enough of their dogs! Keep reading to see some of the most adorable photos and videos of celebrities and their beloved pups.

Here are 30 celebrity dog lovers who just can’t help but show off their love for their pups! You may just find yourself wanting a dog of your own by the end!

1 Miranda Kerr

It turns out that Miranda Kerr, one of the world’s most coveted supermodels, is a bit of a softie when it comes to animals. In a recent interview, Kerr revealed her love for dogs and how her Australian Cattle Dog named Teddy has helped keep her grounded. “I’m obsessed with my dog,” Kerr said. “He’s been by my side through everything – good times and bad.” It seems like Kerr is just like the rest of us; she can’t help but be won to those adorable puppy dog eyes.

2 Emily Ratajkowski

Emily Ratajkowski sure does! The model and actress have been photographed numerous times with her beloved pup, and she always looks happy.

3 James Middleton

Did you know that James Middleton, the brother of Kate Middleton, is a dog lover? He has two rescue dogs named Ella and Mia. In fact, he’s such a big fan of canines that he even launched his own business called The Dog Bakery in London.

4 Jenny Slate

Jenny Slate is known for being a talented actress, but what you may not know is that she’s also a huge dog lover! In fact, her pup Archie is practically her sidekick. Jenny has been vocal about how much she loves dogs, and even uses them as the basis for some of her stand-up comedy routines.

5 Sergey Lazarev

Sergeys rescue dogs Daisy and Lisa are a major part of his life. When at home, he takes them walking every day. Throughout the years, Sergey has homed many dogs. Hes even gone so far as to set up a bespoke pet supplies store in Moscow.

6 Josh Groban

Josh Groban, one of the most successful singers in the world, is a dog lover. In an interview with People magazine, he said that his dog is his “world.” He went on to say that “dogs provide such simple and pure companionship.” Anyone who has ever been around a dog knows how true this is!

7 Lana Condor

Hollywood superstar Lana Condor is best known for her roles in blockbuster films such as “To All the Boys I’ve Loved Before”.By now, you know that Lana Condor and her boyfriend, Anthony de la Torre, adopted the most adorable puppy ever, and named her Emmy.

8 Andy Cohen

Did you know that Andy Cohen, host of Bravo’s Watch What Happens Live and executive producer of The Real Housewives franchise, is a self-proclaimed dog lover? In fact, he has even said that his dog Wacha is his “most favorite person in the world.”

9 Sofia Vergara

Sofia Vergara’s love of dogs is no secret. The actress and her husband, Joe Manganiello, are the proud owners of a rescue dog named Roxy and have been very vocal about their love of animals. In a recent interview, Vergara opened up about her life with Roxy and how the pup has helped her through some tough times.

10 Kate Beckinsale

In a recent interview with W magazine, Kate Beckinsale revealed her love for dogs. She says that they are the “most loyal and uncomplicated of creatures” and that she has two rescue mutts of her own. Her favorite pastime? Taking them on long walks in Los Angeles. I

11 Ariana Grande

Do you love your dog as much as Ariana Grande loves hers? If so, you’ll want to read on about the pop star’s furry little friend. According to Grande, her pup is “the best thing that’s ever happened to me.” And it’s easy to see why the adorable mutt has made appearances on Grande’s Instagram and even accompanied her to the Grammy Award

12 Mickey Rourke

Mickey Rourke is known for his love of dogs. In fact, he has said that “nothing makes me happier than when I’m with my dog.” He loves to take them for long walks and play with them in the park. In fact, he often brings his dog to the set with him while he’s filming. It’s clear that Mickey Rourke loves his dogs, and it’s a joy to see him interact with them.

13 Ashley Tisdale

When it comes to her dogs, Ashley Tisdale doesn’t mess around. The actress and singer is a devoted pet parent to three furry friends, and she’s not afraid to show her love for them on social media. In fact, Tisdale’s Instagram is packed with pics of her pups

14 Katherine Heigl

When Katherine Heigl adopted her dog, she knew that she would have a loyal friend by her side. She didn’t know, however, just how much love and happiness the pup would bring into her life. From playing together in the backyard to cuddling on the couch, Heigl and her dog are the best of friends. The actress even takes her furry companion with her wherever she goes, whether it’s to work or out on the town.

15 Priyanka Chopra And Nick Jonas

We all know that Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas are in love, but we didn’t know that they loved dogs just as much! The two were recently spotted at an animal shelter in Los Angeles and they looked so happy. It’s clear that they have a lot of love for animals!

16 Selena Gomez

Did you know that Selena Gomez loves dogs? She has two of her own, and she even took one of them with her on stage during a performance. In fact, Gomez is such a big fan of dogs that she decided to launch her own line of dog clothes. Called “Selena Gomez x A Dogs Life,” the line includes everything from hoodies to raincoats to bandanas.

17 Nina Dobrev

Nina Dobrev is a huge dog lover, and she proves it by posting lots of pictures of her different dogs on Instagram. Recently, she even shared a photo of herself kissing one of her dogs on the mouth! She may be best known for her work on The Vampire Diaries, but Dobrevs passion for canine companions is definitely worth noting. I

18 Heidi Klum

Heidi Klum loves her dog. She’s been quoted as saying, “I’m a dog person through and through. I grew up with dogs, and my family always had at least one.” Her love for dogs is evident in the way she spoils her own dog, Johan. Read on to find out some of the ways Heidi spoils her furry friend!

19 Sarah Silverman

Sarah Silverman was recently seen out and about in Los Angeles with her beloved dog. The comedian looked happy and content, spending time with her furry friend. Silverman has been a longtime advocate for dogs, and it’s clear that she loves her pet deeply.

20 Lily Collins

Have you ever seen the movie “The Notebook?” If so, then you know who Lily Collins is. She played Noah’s daughter in the movie. Lily Collins is not only an amazing actress, but she’s also a dog lover!

21 Kaley Cuoco

Kaley Cuoco, who is known for her roles in “The Big Bang Theory” and “8 Simple Rules,” loves spending time with her dog. She has even said that her dog is like a family member to her. Kaley often posts pictures of herself with her dog on social media, and she always looks happy when she’s together.

22 George Clooney

George Clooney is one of the most dashing and debonair actors in Hollywood, but what many people may not know is that he loves dogs! In fact, he’s been known to adopt rescue dogs and give them a loving home. Dogs have always played an important role in George Clooney’s life, and it’s clear that he truly enjoys their company.

23 Lionel Messi

Who doesn’t love a good dog story? Lionel Messi, one of the world’s top soccer players and all-around good guy, just adopted a dog and is absolutely smitten with her. Named after Messi’s home country of Argentina, Gauchita has won the heart of her new dad with her adorable looks and playful personality. As if we needed another reason to love Messi!

24 Drew Barrymore

Drew Barrymore is a well-known dog lover, and she has plenty of them. In fact, she’s on the board of directors for the ASPCA, and she’s always been an advocate for animal welfare. She often brings her dogs with her when she’s out and about, and she’s even been known to take them on movie sets. Drew loves dogs for many reasons; they’re loyal, loving, and great companions. Plus, they make great workout partners!

25 Sylvester Stallone

Although Sylvester Stallone is most well-known for his iconic roles in action movies, the star has a soft spot for dogs. In fact, he’s so devoted to canine companions that he launched his own line of dog food called “Sly Dog.” The actor has said that his dogs are like family to him and he loves spending time with them.

26 David Beckham

David Beckham is one of the most well-known soccer players in the world. He’s also a dog lover! Beckham has been photographed many times with his beloved bulldog, named Butch. Butch travels with Beckham wherever he goes and even has his own spot on the team bench. Beckham recently announced that he is stepping down from his professional soccer career, but we’re sure his pup will still be by his side!

27 Danny Trejo

Danny Trejo, the man with the badass attitude and the heavily accentuated mustache, loves dogs. In a recent interview with Jimmy Kimmel, Trejo openly shared his love for our furry friends, going as far as to say that he wants “to have 100 dogs.”

28 Justin Theroux

Who doesn’t love Justin Theroux? The versatile actor is not only ridiculously good-looking but he’s also got a heart of gold, as evidenced by his love for dogs. In a recent interview, Theroux said that he and fiancee Jennifer Aniston have three rescue dogs and they’re the loves of his life.

29 Emilia Clarke

Fans of “Game of Thrones” know actress Emilia Clarke as the powerful Daenerys Targaryen. But what they may not know is that she’s a huge dog lover! In a recent interview, Clarke said that she has two dogs and adores both of them. She even credits her dogs with helping her through some tough times in her life. It’s clear that Clarke is a big animal lover, and we’re just happy to know that she’s such a kind-hearted person.

30 Jackie Chan

There’s no denying that Jackie Chan is one of the most successful action stars in history, but what you may not know is that he’s also a major dog lover! In fact, his devotion to dogs is so great that he’s even started his own dog rescue organization.

We all know, dogs are some of the best companions a person could have. They keep us company when no one else is available and they provide unconditional love to anyone who will take them in. It’s no wonder that celebrities like David Beckham, Jackie Chan, Emilia Clarke, and more would want their own furry friend!

Which celebrity dog lover is your favorite?
