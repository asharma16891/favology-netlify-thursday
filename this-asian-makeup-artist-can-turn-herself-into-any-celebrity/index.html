A makeup artist named gillianisme on TikTok is transforming herself into virtually any celebrity or recognizable character with brushes and shadows. She has transformed herself into the likes of Scarlett Johansson (2017), Scarlet Witch from Avengers Age Of Ultron; Kate Blanchett as Elizabeth I in “Into The Woods” – she even manages to make Anne Hathaway look good.

When people are alarmed by how easy it is to fake anything with digital means, like Photoshop, and despite the fact that nobody can surprise anyone anymore because everyone knows this too. But this makeup artist shows us otherwise; he demonstrates how one could create an incredible false identity using more “analog” methods such as brushes for shadowing or mascara.
